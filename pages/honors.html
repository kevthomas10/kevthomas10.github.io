Gateway to University Honors

Gateway to University Honors is a course deisgned to help understand the requirements of the University Honors Program and the benefits of participation. This is accomplished through a series of reflective assignments and discussions.

Values Reflection

The value reflection is used to reflect on past experiences and see how my values have effected decisions I have made and to also help me determine what values are most important to me.


Passions Reflection

A reflection about my passions and how I got interested in it and where I want to go with it. I hope you enjoy reading it.

Mock Honors Self-designed Proposal

A self-deigned trip to San Fransisco to explore the technology culture of the city and surrounding areas. As a University of Cincinnati Honors Student, I have the ability to design my own honors trip and receive partial funding to go on the trip. I hope you enjoy reading my proposal.
