About Me


Hello! My name is Kevin Thomas and I am a second year in the Lindner Honors-PLUS program studying Industrial Management at the University of Cincinnati. I am involved on campus through Undergraduate Student Government and the Lindner Student Association. I am the director of technology in both organizations.

I am not a Cincinnati native (I grew up in Hilliard, Ohio), in fact, until freshman orientation I had never spent more than a half day in Cincinnati. However, I have come to call Cincinnati home through my first year at UC and love being a Cincinnatian.

I am very close with my family and enjoy spending time with them, so the fact that UC is under two hours away from home was a major factor in coming to UC. Another factor in attending UC was my acceptance into the Lindner Honors-PLUS program and the benefits it provides throughout my five years at UC. One of the immediate benefits was 22 classmates in the program with me. However, those 22 classmates turned into my family away from home and I look forward to four more years of college with all of them.

In my free-time I enjoy reading anything and everything about technology and watching football. I go to UC and live in Cincinnati but I am a passionate Ohio State and Pittsburgh Steelers fan (I know), but I was born and raised a fan of the teams and am extremely loyal to them.

If you made it this far thank you! Please explore the rest of my site to learn more about my life!
